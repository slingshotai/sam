# EP Knowledge Base — Overview

The EP Knowledge Base is your searchable archive of 283+ eCommerce Podcast episodes, tagged and indexed against the Slingshot Framework.

## What's In It

Every episode has been tagged with:
- **Slingshot areas** — which of the 7 framework areas it covers (Sell, Story, Tech Stack, Marketing, Optimise, Experience, Growth)
- **FUEL stages** — the marketing maturity level of the tactics discussed (Foundation, Unlock, Elevate, Leapfrog)
- **Website menu tags** — specific topics from the EP website taxonomy
- **Key takeaway** — the single most actionable insight in one sentence
- **Related skills** — which SlingshotAI skills connect to the episode content

## Why It Matters

The EP archive contains hundreds of hours of real-world ecommerce expertise — founder stories, agency strategies, expert interviews. But it's only useful if you can find the right episode at the right time.

The knowledge base turns "I vaguely remember someone talking about referrals on the podcast" into "Jay Myers on EP 283 showed that a 0.4 increase in referral rate produced 56x subscriber growth — that maps to the Growth area at Foundation level."

## How to Use It

Ask SAM or Claude:
- "What EP episodes cover email marketing?"
- "Find me case studies about subscription businesses"
- "Who talked about product page conversion on EP?"
- "What episodes are relevant to my Moby audit score?"
- "Show me Foundation-level episodes about referral programmes"

The knowledge base searches across titles, guests, tags, takeaways, and framework areas to find the most relevant episodes. It can return quick summaries or pull up the full episode content for deeper reading.

## How It Grows

New episodes are tagged and added to the index as they're published. The archive gets more valuable over time — more examples, more case studies, more evidence for every area of the Slingshot Framework.
